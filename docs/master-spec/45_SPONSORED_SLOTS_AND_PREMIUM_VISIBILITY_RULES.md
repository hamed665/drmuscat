# 45 — Sponsored Slots and Premium Visibility Rules V10.2

Premium visibility must be transparent and safe. Paid providers must not silently replace organic relevance.

## Canonical Listing Structure

A category/area listing page may show:

1. Featured Partners / Sponsored slots
2. Organic results
3. Optional mid-list sponsored slot after organic item 10
4. Related content and internal links

## Sponsored Labeling

Every paid placement must show a visible label:

- English: Sponsored
- Arabic: إعلان ممول

No design may hide, minimize, or obscure the label.

## Slot Caps

Default caps:

- top sponsored cards: max 3
- mid-list sponsored cards: max 1 after first 10 organic results
- homepage hero: max 1 active sponsor per rotation slot
- article footer sponsored providers: max 3 relevant providers

## Organic Ranking

Organic ranking uses relevance signals:

- profile completeness
- verified status
- review quality
- recent updates
- service/category match
- area/city match
- opening status
- language match
- distance if available
- engagement quality

Paid plan may grant sponsored inventory, richer profile capabilities, and analytics. It must not silently make a weak listing the #1 organic result.

## Admin Config

Admins may configure placement keys, slot count, country, city, category, area, pricing mode, start/end dates, and eligibility. All changes must be audit logged.
