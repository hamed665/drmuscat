# DrMuscat V10.1 — SEO-First Business Model and Access Revision

## Purpose
V10.1 changed the business direction from a card/membership-driven model into an SEO-first, free-discovery, provider-funded platform.

## Main Decisions

```text
- Public browsing must be free and anonymous.
- User registration remains free.
- Persian/Hindi are not launch SEO routes.
- English and Arabic are launch languages.
- Health Card/card sales are removed from the business model.
- Patient offers replace card-based discounts.
- Provider subscriptions become the primary monetization model.
- Sponsored visibility is allowed only when clearly labeled.
- Organic ranking must not be sold.
```

## Implementation Impact

```text
- Do not build Health Card flows.
- Do not require login for public discovery.
- Do not generate fa/hi public SEO routes at launch.
- Build provider plan support.
- Build patient offers as the replacement discount mechanism.
```
