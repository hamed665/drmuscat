02

Typography

English uses Spectral (serif display) + Manrope (UI). Arabic uses Alexandria for headings (smaller, lighter) + IBM Plex Sans Arabic for body/UI.

Display heading.display · EN Spectral 400 clamp(40–64) · AR Alexandria 500 clamp(35–53)
Section heading.h2 · EN Spectral · AR Alexandria 500, smaller
Card heading.h3
Lead paragraph for intros and hero subtitles..lead · AR line-height 1.9

09

RTL & mobile rules

Arabic / RTL

  • dir=rtl flips layout, nav, cards & icons
  • Arabic headings smaller & lighter
  • Latin names & numerals stay LTR
  • Arabic body line-height ≈ 1.9

Mobile

  • No horizontal scroll; search stacks
  • Filters become a full-screen sheet
  • Touch targets ≥ 44px
  • Carousels swipe with snap
